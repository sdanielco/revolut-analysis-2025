Israeli Market

Entry into Israel

Israel is identified as a strategic market — a concentrated financial sector where banks earned ₪30B profit in 2024, and customers are actively seeking alternatives.

2023
Identification Code #78
Revolut received identification code from Bank of Israel — the first step to becoming an official player in the local market.
August 2024
Official Banking Number
Bank of Israel assigned Revolut an official banking number — a significant step toward becoming a licensed banking player.
July 2025
Payment License Granted
Israel's Securities Authority granted Revolut a payment license alongside Rapyd, Airwallex, and Mesh — including holding customer funds and FX.
2025 — In progress
Lean Bank Application
Revolut in active dialogue with Bank of Israel for a lean banking license — which would allow accepting deposits and extending credit.
2026+
Full Banking Launch
With Lean Bank approval — Revolut will compete directly with Israeli banks on deposits, credit, and foreign exchange.

Threats to the Israeli Financial Market

Low Fees
Puts pressure on the profits of Israeli banks that earned ₪30B in 2024. Revolut offers a fraction of the fees charged by traditional banks.
Competitive FX Rates
Competitive exchange rates compared to any Israeli bank. An Israeli traveling abroad can save thousands of shekels annually.
High-Yield Deposits
Israeli banks offer 0.01% on deposits while the Bank of Israel rate is 4.5%. Lean Bank will allow Revolut to attract deposits with competitive interest.
Youth-First Strategy
Gen Z and Millennials demand a digital-first experience. Revolut is perfectly tailored for this segment, which traditional banks struggle to retain.

Strategic Response: The Bit Playbook

A data-driven defensive strategy for Bit — three high-impact initiatives and a four-quarter execution roadmap to defend market leadership against Revolut's entry.

15%
FX Wallet
Retention KPI — International Travelers
Launch a multi-currency virtual card with real-time FX at interbank rates. Target: retain 15% of Bit's 3.5M users who currently switch to Revolut when traveling. Each retained user = ₪180 annual revenue uplift.
+20%
Smart Cashback
DAU Growth Target
AI-driven cashback engine connected to 50 top Israeli retail chains. Users earn rewards passively — no manual activation. Projected +20% Daily Active Users within 90 days of launch, turning Bit into a daily-use app.
1.4×
Group Payments
Viral Coefficient Target
Shared expenses, trip splits, and group collections — each new group session invites non-Bit users organically. Target viral coefficient of 1.4: every user brings 1.4 new users, turning the existing network into a growth engine.

מינוף ה-Network Effect הקיים לפני שרבולוט מגיעה למסה קריטית
לביט כבר יש 3.5M משתמשים ו-90% משוק ה-P2P — יתרון מבני ש-Revolut לא יכולה לשכפל בין לילה. כל חודש של חוסר פעולה מאפשר ל-Revolut לצבור 40,000+ משתמשים ישראלים ולהתקרב לנקודת ה-Tipping Point שבה אפקטי הרשת מתהפכים. חלון הפעולה הוא 12–18 חודשים, לא 3–5 שנים.
02
הפיכת "הישראליות" ל-Competitive Moat — לא תירוץ לאי-פעולה
אינטגרציית Open Banking עם הבנקים הישראליים, UX בעברית מלאה, תאימות רגולטורית, ואמון מותג מקומי הם יתרונות מבניים ש-Revolut תבלה 18+ חודשים בניסיון להשיג. ביט חייבת לנצל אלה עכשיו — בניית אינטגרציות עמוקות עם נתוני בנק הפועלים, ביטוח לאומי ורשתות המסחר המקומיות לפני שרבולוט תשלים את ההתקנות המקומיות שלה.
03
הפעלת מנוע ההכנסות: המרת נפח P2P להכנסות ממוצרים פרימיום
ביט מעבדת ₪29.4B בשנה — אך כל שקל עובר ברמת הכנסות אפסית. המרת רק 20% מהמשתמשים הפעילים לשירותי פרימיום (FX Wallet, קאשבק, PFM, השקעות) ב-₪59/חודש תייצר ₪247M הכנסות חוזרות שנתיות — מספיק להגיע לרווחיות תפעולית ולסגור את ההפסד המצטבר של ₪700M תוך 3 שנים.

Conclusions & Outlook

Key takeaways from the analysis and what to expect in Israel's financial market.

01
Revolut Is a Growth Machine
72% revenue growth, 38% customer growth, $1.4B profit — Revolut has proven it's possible to build a profitable bank with zero branches. At $75B valuation, it is one of the most powerful fintechs in the world.
02
Israel — A Strategic Market
Concentrated financial market, banks with high margins, customers actively seeking alternatives. The payment license (July 2025) is the first step in a future takeover.
03
Bit Must Innovate
Bit dominates P2P payments, but lacks product diversity. Fees introduced in 2025 weaken its positioning. Without rapid innovation, it will lose young customers.
04
The Winner — Israeli Consumers
Competition will bring higher deposit rates, lower transfer fees, and better financial services. The financial revolution is coming to Israel.
